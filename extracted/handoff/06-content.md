## Voice rules
- No emoji.
- No exclamation marks (replace with periods).
- Avoid: "revolutionary", "next-gen", "cutting-edge", "unleash", "unlock", "supercharge", "game-changer", "transform" (used by every AI vendor — banned), "leverage", "synergy".
- Prefer specifics over claims: numbers, named outcomes, named systems, named timelines.
- Sentences run 12–22 words on average. Cut anything that doesn't pull weight.
- The em-dash is fine. Use it.
